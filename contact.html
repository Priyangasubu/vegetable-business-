Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to all areas within a 30-mile radius of our facility. This includes Fresh City and surrounding suburbs. For locations outside this range, please contact us for special arrangements.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by filling out the contact form on this page, calling our customer service number, or sending us an email. We'll confirm your order within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash on delivery. For regular customers, we also offer monthly subscription plans with automatic payments.

How fresh are your vegetables?

Our vegetables are harvested on the same day or one day before delivery, ensuring maximum freshness. We work directly with local farmers to minimize the time between harvest and delivery.

Do you offer organic vegetables?

Yes, we offer a range of certified organic vegetables. These are clearly labeled in our product listings and come with a slight premium due to the specialized farming practices required.
